## 3D Modeling Repositories 

Repositories are community supported hosts containing files typically associated with 3D Printing such as .stl .3mf as well as some STEP files

<Tabs.Tab key="Maker World">
Hosted by Bambu Labs
- Notable: Contains ready to print .3mf files & g-code for their printers. 

- Maker Supplies: Ready made electronics and motors for several projects 

- Contests: Community modeling contests diferent areas such a Hiking, and City of the Future. 

- Maker Lab: Several generative modeling tools.

- Model types: Free 
![makerworld.webp](https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/JamesD3d/info3dimages@main/i/3d_Files_Sources/makerworld.webp)
