## 3D Modeling Repositories 

Repositories are community supported hosts containing files typically associated with 3D Printing such as .stl .3mf as well as some STEP files

Hosted by Bambu Labs
- Notable: Contains ready to print .3mf files & g-code for their printers.

- Maker Lab: Several generative modeling tools.
