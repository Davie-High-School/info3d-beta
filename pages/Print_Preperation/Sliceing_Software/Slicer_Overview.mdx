<Tabs.Tab key="Orca Slicer">
### Orca Slicer

Orca Slicer is a high-performance slicer with advanced tuning for Bambu Lab printers.

![Orca_Slicer](public/issues/orca-slicer_HTNPG0TVTR.gif)

</Tabs.Tab>

<Tabs.Tab key="Bambu Studio">
### Bambu Studio

Bambu Studio is the official slicer from Bambu Lab, designed for optimal performance with their ecosystem.

![Bambu_Slicer]()

</Tabs.Tab>

<Tabs.Tab key="Prusa Slicer">
### Prusa Slicer

Developed by Prusa Research, this slicer is known for feature-rich controls and excellent support generation.

![Prusa_Slicer]()

</Tabs.Tab>

<Tabs.Tab key="Super Slicer">
### Super Slicer

Super Slicer is a fork of Prusa Slicer, geared towards advanced users with fine-tuned calibration controls.

![Super_Slicer]()

</Tabs.Tab>

<Tabs.Tab key="Slic3r">
### Slic3r

The original open-source slicer that inspired many modern slicers. Lightweight and extensible.

![Slic3r_Slicer]()

</Tabs.Tab>

<Tabs.Tab key="Kiri:Moto">
### Kiri:Moto

A browser-based slicer great for quick slicing tasks and integrating into web workflows.

![Kiri_Slicer]()

</Tabs.Tab>

<Tabs.Tab key="Cura">
### Cura

One of the most popular slicers, maintained by Ultimaker. Known for broad printer support.

![Cura_Slicer]()

</Tabs.Tab>

<Tabs.Tab key="IdeaMaker">
### IdeaMaker

IdeaMaker is a versatile slicer by Raise3D, useful even outside their printer line.

![IdeaMaker_Slicer]()

</Tabs.Tab>

</Tabs>
