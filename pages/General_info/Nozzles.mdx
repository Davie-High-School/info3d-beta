## Nozzle and Layer Height Guide
Model printing times are most affected by a cobonation of layer heright and nozzle size. 
- The majority of 3d printers will come standard with a 0.4 mm nozzle made of brass. A good middle ground between qualty and time is 0.2 mm layer heights. 



<Callout>
# Recomendaton  
| Nozzle Size  | Layer Height |
|--------------|--------------|
| 0.4 mm       | 0.2 mm       |

</Callout>

| 0.2 mm      | 0.06 mm          | ~90 min                | 0.14 mm          | ~45 min                | 0.22 mm                | 0.3 mm                 |
