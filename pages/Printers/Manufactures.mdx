<Tabs.Tab key="Anycubic">

**Anycubic** is known for offering budget-friendly FDM and resin 3D printers with strong community support. Popular models include the Kobra and Photon series.</Tabs.Tab>

<Tabs.Tab key="Bambu Labs">
[Visit Bambu Labs](URL_PLACEHOLDER)

**Bambu Labs** produces high-speed, closed-loop 3D printers like the X1C and P1S series, featuring AI-based monitoring, CoreXY design, and automatic filament switching.</Tabs.Tab>

<Tabs.Tab key="Creality">
[Visit Creality](URL_PLACEHOLDER)

**Creality** is one of the most widely used consumer 3D printer brands, known for models like the Ender and CR series, offering affordable and modifiable machines.</Tabs.Tab>

<Tabs.Tab key="Elegoo">
[Visit Elegoo](URL_PLACEHOLDER)

**Elegoo** specializes in high-resolution resin printers like the Mars and Saturn series, aimed at hobbyists and prosumers looking for fine detail.</Tabs.Tab>

<Tabs.Tab key="Flsun">
[Visit Flsun](URL_PLACEHOLDER)

**Flsun** focuses on delta-style 3D printers known for their fast print speeds and tall build volumes, like the Super Racer (SR).</Tabs.Tab>

<Tabs.Tab key="Flash Forge">
[Visit Flash Forge](URL_PLACEHOLDER)

**FlashForge** offers reliable plug-and-play FDM printers for education and prosumer use, such as the Adventurer and Creator series.</Tabs.Tab>

<Tabs.Tab key="Sovol">
[Visit Sovol](URL_PLACEHOLDER)

**Sovol** provides feature-rich, budget-friendly FDM printers often equipped with direct drive extruders and dual Z-axis support.</Tabs.Tab>

<Tabs.Tab key="Prusa">
[Visit Prusa](URL_PLACEHOLDER)

**Prusa Research** is known for its high-quality, open-source printers like the Original Prusa i3 MK4 and Prusa MINI, with exceptional community and support.</Tabs.Tab>
